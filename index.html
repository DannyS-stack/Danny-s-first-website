Things I like

Dungeons & Dragons

You might have heard of it, or not. It's the most popular "tabletop roleplaying game" but if you dont know it that still doesn't say much. You basically create a character with certain strengths and weaknesses and roll dice to overcome problems. Its kind of like playing an rpg on the computer, but sitting around a table with actual people makes it just so much more fun.

Meditation

I practice meditation and yoga daily, I feel like meditation really improves quality of life by making you more calm and focussed. The point of meditation is to become more aware of your thoughts and the goings-on of your mind. It allows you to see whats going on in your mind without getting too involved in it. It's a great thing and it's getting increasily more popular. I also like to read the philosphy of the religions associated with yoga and meditation but would not call myself religious.

Coding

A very recent addition to my array of things I like, I only found out that I liked this during lockdown. Always thought you had to be some math genius or tech-wiz to understand it and never looked twice at it. Turns out it can be taught to mere mortals. So here I am.

warhammer 40k

Another Tabletop game, a "tabletop wargame" this time. Very different from Dungeons and Dragons mentioned earlier. In this hobby you collect expensive miniatures from one of many stylistically and functionally different armies and play a tactical wargame against another human doing the same.

miniature painting

The miniatures that I bought to play warhammer 40k introduced me to another hobby, miniature painting. It's closely tied with warhammer 40k but it's an entire hobby in and of itself. I have gotten to a pretty decent level of painting myself but there are miniature painters out there who create nothing less than art. Check out the winners of the Golden Demon competition.

Reading

I also love reading, but I have too many hobbies and don't spend much time on this. I usually read in public transport when going to work or school. I also enjoy listening to audiobooks while painting miniatures. I mostly read fantasy or science fiction books and sometimes non fiction if its a subject I'm interested in.
